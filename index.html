About Us

Welcome to Ecotechpro Waste Management Solutions

Ecotechpro Waste Management Solutions is a leading waste management organization committed to promoting environmental sustainability...

Ecotechpro Waste Management Solutions is a leading waste management organization committed to promoting environmental sustainability and providing professional, innovative, and efficient waste management services to communities and businesses. We operate with a focus on improving environmental health, enhancing community well-being, and fostering sustainable living practices.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted waste management partner, inspiring sustainable living and creating environmentally safe communities.

Our Mission

To deliver innovative waste management solutions through exceptional service delivery, community engagement, and environmental conservation, ensuring cleaner and healthier living environments for all.

Core Values

  • Sustainability: Advocating for environmentally friendly practices.
  • Accountability: Transparency in service delivery and operations.
  • Community Engagement: Building partnerships to achieve shared goals.
  • Innovation: Embracing cutting-edge technologies for efficient waste management.

How We Operate

1. Service Offerings

  • Door-to-Door Waste Collection:
    • Pickup days: Wednesdays and Sundays starting at 7:00 AM.
    • Waste Sorting Requirements:
      • Organic Waste: Deposited in bins provided within the premises.
      • Recyclables (e.g., plastics, glass, paper): Disposed of in biodegradable bags provided by Ecotechpro.
      • Non-Recyclables (e.g., non-biodegradable materials): Placed in separate biodegradable bags.
  • Private Pickup Services: For clients requiring customized waste collection services outside the regular schedule.

2. Payment Structure

  • Door-to-Door Collection: KES 300 per household monthly.
  • Private Pickup Services: KES 600 per session.
  • Payment Mode: Integrated with monthly rent and payable to landlords/property management by the 10th of every month.

3. Additional Services

  • Environmental Cleanups: Organizing community-led cleanup initiatives in collaboration with local authorities.
  • Waste Education Campaigns: Educating clients and communities on proper waste disposal and recycling.
  • Long-Term Environmental Security: Measures to prevent illegal waste dumping by outsiders, supported by a KES 1,500 monthly fee per landlord.

Institutional Structure

Ecotechpro Waste Management Solutions operates with a skilled and dedicated team:

  • Founders: Strategic management and quality control.
  • Logistics Team: Route optimization and waste transport coordination.
  • Pickup Agents: On-ground collection and sorting.
  • Sales and Marketing Team: Customer engagement and service promotion.
  • Support Staff: Administrative and client support services.

Policies and Guidelines

  • Waste Placement: All sorted waste should be placed at the doorstep by 7:00 AM on pickup days.
  • Proper Sorting: Clients must separate organic, recyclable, and non-recyclable waste as instructed.
  • Timely Payments: Late payments result in service suspension until dues are cleared.
  • Compliance: Landlords and property managers ensure all tenants adhere to waste policies.

Technology Integration

  • Mobile Payment Systems: Payment through Paybill for added convenience.
  • Client Management Tools: Tracking service requests, payments, and feedback.
  • Website Development Features:
    • Client Portal: Personalized accounts for tracking payment history, scheduling services, and submitting inquiries.
    • Service Updates: Timely notifications for pickups, cleanups, and policy changes.
    • Educational Resources: Waste management tips and sustainability guides.

Future Goals

  • Expansion: Reaching underserved areas and expanding our service network.
  • Advanced Technologies: Adopting AI and IoT for waste tracking and management.
  • Sustainability Projects: Partnering with government agencies, NGOs, and corporates for green initiatives.
  • Community Empowerment: Creating job opportunities and training for local youth in waste management and environmental conservation.
